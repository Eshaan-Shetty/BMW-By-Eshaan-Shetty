PERFORMANCE FLEET'S


BMW M2 Competition

The BMW M2 Competition is a compact, high-performance sports coupe that builds on the classic M2 formula with more power, sharper handling, and race-inspired engineering. Its aggressive styling and muscular stance emphasize agility and precision, while the 3.0L TwinPower Turbo inline-6 delivers 405 hp, enabling exhilarating acceleration. With rear-wheel drive, a refined suspension, and M-tuned brakes, the M2 Competition combines raw track capability with everyday usability, making it a driver-focused icon in BMW’s M lineup.

BMW M3 Competition

The BMW M3 Competition is a high-performance sports sedan that blends everyday usability with race-track dynamics. Equipped with a 3.0L TwinPower Turbo inline-6 producing 510 hp, and xDrive all-wheel drive, it achieves blistering acceleration while maintaining remarkable composure in corners. Its athletic body, flared wheel arches, and signature M design cues convey both power and sophistication. The M3 Competition’s advanced chassis, adaptive suspension, and precision steering make it a perfect combination of performance, technology, and luxury.

BMW M3 Touring

The BMW M3 Touring is a high-performance wagon that blends track-ready dynamics with everyday practicality. Its 3.0-litre twin-turbo inline-six engine produces up to 530 hp and 650 Nm of torque, enabling 0–100 km/h in approximately 3.3 seconds. The M xDrive all-wheel-drive system provides exceptional grip, while the adaptive M suspension ensures sharp handling and stability. With aggressive styling, advanced driver-focused technology, and versatile cargo space, the M3 Touring delivers a perfect balance of performance and usability.

BMW M4 Competition

The BMW M4 Competition is a high-performance sports coupe that represents the pinnacle of M engineering for two-door performance enthusiasts. Powered by a 3.0L TwinPower Turbo inline-6 producing 510 hp, and equipped with xDrive all-wheel drive, it delivers rapid acceleration and razor-sharp handling. The M4 Competition’s aggressive styling, including aerodynamic front bumper, sculpted hood, and flared wheel arches, emphasizes both dynamic capability and modern sophistication. Its precision suspension, advanced braking, and driver-focused cockpit make it ideal for spirited driving on road or track.

BMW M5 Competition (G90)

The BMW M5 Competition (G90) is a luxury performance sedan that combines extreme power with everyday refinement. Its hybrid powertrain produces 717 hp and 1,000 Nm of torque, achieving 0–100 km/h in approximately 3.4 seconds. The xDrive all-wheel-drive system ensures optimal traction while the adaptive M suspension delivers confidence in corners. With bold design, advanced technology, and exceptional comfort, the M5 Competition offers a seamless blend of performance and luxury.

BMW M5 Touring

The BMW M5 Touring is a luxury performance wagon that combines electrified power with everyday versatility. Its plug-in hybrid powertrain produces a staggering 717 hp and 1,000 Nm of torque, accelerating from 0–100 km/h in around 3.5 seconds. The M xDrive all-wheel-drive system enhances traction and control, while adaptive M suspension provides a refined yet dynamic driving experience. With bold design, cutting-edge technology, and generous interior space, the M5 Touring seamlessly merges supercar performance with premium comfort.

BMW M8 Competition

The BMW M8 Competition is a flagship high-performance luxury coupe and convertible that perfectly balances raw power with sophisticated technology. Its 4.4L M TwinPower Turbo V8 engine produces 617 hp, paired with xDrive all-wheel drive for exceptional traction and a 0–100 km/h time of around 3.2 seconds. The M8 Competition’s aggressive styling emphasizes both elegance and athleticism. Inside, premium materials and advanced M-specific controls combine with adaptive suspension and performance brakes to deliver supercar-level dynamics with everyday usability.

BMW X5 M Competition

The BMW X5 M Competition is a high-performance luxury SUV that delivers exceptional power and dynamic driving capabilities. Its 4.4-litre twin-turbo V8 engine produces 625 hp and 750 Nm of torque, achieving 0–100 km/h in approximately 3.8 seconds. The M xDrive all-wheel-drive system ensures maximum traction and control, while adaptive M suspension enhances handling and ride comfort. With its bold design, advanced technology, and spacious interior, the X5 M Competition offers a perfect blend of performance and practicality.

BMW XM Label Red

The BMW XM Label Red is the high-performance flagship SUV from BMW M, combining luxury, hybrid power, and aggressive styling. It delivers extreme performance while maintaining premium comfort and advanced technology. Its bold proportions, sharp design lines, and muscular stance emphasize both elegance and power, making it a standout model in the modern BMW M lineup. Powered by a 4.4L twin-turbo V8 engine combined with an electric motor, it produces an exceptional 748 hp and 1,000 Nm of torque, delivering 0–100 km/h in approximately 3.8 seconds.
